Frequently Asked Questions

How do the live sessions work?

Classes are held via Zoom/Teams integration. You can interact in real-time with instructors. If you miss a session, recordings are available on your dashboard within 2 hours.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all self-paced courses. For bootcamp tracks, you can withdraw within the first week for a full refund.

Do I get a certificate?

Absolutely. Upon successful completion of quizzes and capstone projects, you receive a digital certificate verifiable via LinkedIn.

Can I access courses offline?

Yes! Our mobile app allows you to download video lectures and reading materials for offline access, so you can learn on the go without an internet connection.

Do you offer corporate training?

We partner with Fortune 500 companies to upskill their workforce. Contact our sales team for custom enterprise packages and bulk enrollment discounts.

Is there a community forum?

Every course includes access to a private Slack community where you can collaborate with peers, ask mentors questions, and network with alumni.
